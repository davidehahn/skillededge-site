Common questions

Before you reach out.

What data do you actually need from us?
WMS labor activity exports (pick timestamps, associate IDs, order volumes), payroll records (hours by associate, OT hours, dates), and billing records for the last 3–6 months. We send you a one-page Data Spec Sheet on day one that tells your WMS admin exactly what to pull and how to format it.
We don't have clean data — will that be a problem?
Mid-market 3PLs rarely do. If your data doesn't meet the spec, we'll tell you immediately and work with your WMS admin to get it formatted correctly before we start the clock. If the data cleanup requires significant time, a data-cleansing surcharge may apply — but we flag that upfront, not after the fact.
How does the guarantee actually work?
Simple. After delivering findings, if the total identified recoverable waste across all five areas is less than $75,000 annually, we void the invoice. You keep all deliverables. No questions asked. We've never had to void one.

No. The audit is entirely remote and asynchronous. Your WMS admin pulls the data exports (20–30 minutes). You join two calls totaling 90 minutes. Everything else happens on our end. Your floor never knows an audit is happening.

You receive the report, checklists, and toolkit. That's the engagement — no ongoing fees, no retainer, no follow-up upsell pressure. If you want help implementing the findings on-site, we offer a separate one-day floor engagement at $3,500. But that's entirely your call after seeing the results.
