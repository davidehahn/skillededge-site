Common Questions
Before You Book a Call
Is this a compliance tool? Will it cause legal issues?
No. MedVault is a training and systematization platform — it documents how YOU already perform treatments so your staff can follow the same process. It is not medical advice, not a compliance product, and does not replace your medical director's oversight or your state's licensing requirements. We recommend every clinic have their protocols reviewed by their own medical director.
What if my staff aren't tech-savvy?
The Vault works like any app on their phone — tap, watch, read, answer a few questions. If your staff can use Instagram, they can use MedVault. Most owners report staff are fully comfortable within the first session.
How long does setup actually take?
The extraction session — where Dave captures your protocols — takes about 60 minutes. Your full Vault (SOPs, checklists, quizzes, AI assistant) is built and delivered within 5 to 7 business days after that.
What if I only have 2 staff members? Is this worth it?
Yes — Vault Starter is built exactly for solo owners adding their first or second hire. The sooner you document your protocols, the less painful every future hire becomes.
Can I cancel if it's not working for me?
Yes. You can cancel your monthly subscription anytime after month 3. And if we don't find at least $10,000 in annual recoverable value in your first 30 days, your setup fee is fully refunded.
