How It Works
Three Steps to a Clinic That Runs Your Way

No complicated software. No lengthy implementations. Just your protocols, systematized and ready for your team.

1
Record

You spend 60 minutes walking through your top treatments on camera. Your technique. Your products. Your exact process. Nothing scripted — just you doing what you already do.

2
Build

Our AI system converts your recordings into complete staff training materials: written SOPs, step-by-step checklists, video training clips, and staff competency quizzes. All formatted and ready to use.

3
Train

Your staff access their personalized MedVault on any device. They watch, they study, they pass the quiz. New hires go from day one to confident in two weeks instead of two months.
